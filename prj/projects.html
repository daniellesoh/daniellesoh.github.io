Smart Calcifer Trash Bin

For my final project, I plan to make an automatic trash bin which flips open when a person is near the trash bin using a ultrasonic sensor, with a design of Calcifer for the lid; one of the characters in an animation movie called "Howl's Moving Castle". I would like to have the bin light up after the trash has been thrown and the lid closed.

via GIPHY

Research

Here are the references I have used for this project; the first one being the main one while the other is less referenced:

  • How To Make An Automatic Object Sensing Smart Dustbin - DIY Project

  • "Automatic Trash Bin"

For the lights, I plan on using two neopixel strips that would light up after the lid of the trash bin has closed. These will be placed on the inside of the trash bin.
